 TEACHERS

                    


This WebQuest aims to help students practice language forms (modals or else) that they have studied
by experiencing how they can arrange a trip using WWW sources.
The activity is appropriate for intermediate-level ESL/EFL learners.
It also aims to motivate students to use English through activities  like video presentation or contest.
All of these objectives are aimed to be achieved in a meaningful way, and
students can encounter and experience such activities in their real life as well.

WebQuests having a similar format most of the time help learners a lot in various meaningful ways and
this very template and activity could also be applied into different ESL or SLA settings.

Special thanks to Mahir Sarıgül at Ozyegin University, Istanbul for his web-activity which has given me
the idea to create this WebQuest.
